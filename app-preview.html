What it does

One app replaces five

Budget & Bills
Track income, expenses, actuals. Import bank CSV. AI auto-categorises your transactions.
Meal Planning
Weekly grid for breakfast, lunch, dinner. AI suggests meals by cuisine, price, dietary needs. Auto-generates shopping lists.
School Lunchbox
Per-child profiles with allergies and preferences. AI plans a full week of lunches in one tap. Nut-free, dairy-free — respected.
Vehicles
Track rego, insurance, service history. Traffic-light alerts when things are due. Costs auto-log to budget.
Documents
Insurance policies, warranties, passports — with expiry tracking. Know where everything is stored.
Maintenance
Gutters, pest control, AC filters. Set intervals, get reminders. Quick-add presets for common tasks.
Financial Health
Score out of 100 across 5 dimensions. Tap the grade for a breakdown. "Fix it" guides walk you through improving.
Smart Notifications
5 notification styles. Urgent items surface to the top. Context-aware banners on relevant pages. Guided walkthroughs.
AI Throughout
Daily briefing, meal suggestions, shopping lists, bank categorisation, cost forecasting, calorie estimates — all powered by AI.
