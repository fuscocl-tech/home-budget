☀️
Today tab
Routines, chores and lunchbox — exactly as now. Prizes moved out to its own tab so Today stays focused on what needs doing right now.
📅
Calendar tab
Week strip at top, day schedule below. Routines appear at their trigger time as expandable cards — tap to tick steps. Events and chores ordered below. Early Readers get 7-day only (no month toggle).
🏆
Prizes tab
Full prize store with balance, prize list, and redemption history. Approval notifications appear here. Badge on the tab icon when there's a new approval or available prize.
🔔
Badge on Prizes tab
Red badge on the Prizes tab icon when there are unread approval notifications or newly affordable prizes — draws the child's attention without interrupting the Today flow.
🧒
Age consistency
Early Readers (5–8), Independents (8–12) and Tweens (12–16) all get the same three-tab nav. Only Tiny Tots (1–5) remain Today-only with no nav bar.
🌙
Tweens palette
Dark nav bar (#0f172a) with muted icon colours and a purple active indicator — matches the mature dark header aesthetic, not cartoonish.
