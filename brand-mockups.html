Tone of Voice

Do: "You're on track this month with a healthy buffer."
Don't: "Budget surplus calculated: $1,240.00 remaining."

Warm, conversational, like a friend who's good with money. Not a bank statement.

Do: "Pest control is due — want to mark it done?"
Don't: "WARNING: Maintenance item overdue. Action required."

Gentle nudges, not alarms. The app helps, it doesn't nag.

Do: "Chicken stir-fry tonight. Missing 2 items."
Don't: "Meal plan: 2/3 ingredients unavailable in shopping list."

Practical and specific. Tell them what to do, not what's wrong.

Iconography

No emojis in the core UI (action cards, buttons, navigation). Emojis allowed in user-facing content labels (meal plan categories, document categories, maintenance presets) where they add warmth. Use Lucide-style stroke icons (1.8px weight) for navigation and actions.

Colour Usage Rules

Teal (#0d9488)Primary actions, active states, section labels, links, FAB
Red (#ef4444)Overdue items, over budget, danger actions, expired badges
Amber (#f59e0b)Due soon, warnings, approaching limits
Green (#10b981)On track, income, surplus, completed, healthy
Cream (#faf8f5)Page background — warmer than pure white, easier on the eyes
