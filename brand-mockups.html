Design System

Minimal specs. Let the whitespace do the work.

Primary accent#DC2626 (Ruby Red) — buttons, FAB, links, active states, logo O's
Text#18181b (Zinc 900) — headings and body. Nothing lighter for primary text.
Muted#a1a1aa (Zinc 400) — secondary labels, timestamps, metadata
Borders#e4e4e7 (Zinc 200) — thin, subtle. 1px everywhere.
Background#fafafa or #ffffff — no colour tints on the page background
Status#DC2626 (overdue/danger) · #f59e0b (warning) · #10b981 (on track)
Border radiusCards: 12px · Pills: 99px · Modals: 20px · Inputs: 8px · FAB: 50%
ShadowsCards: 0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.06). Elevated: 0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.08). Minimal.
TypographySystem font stack. 900 for page titles, 800 for section labels, 600 for card titles, 400 for body.
IconsLucide-style, 2px stroke, #18181b. No emojis in core UI.
The red ruleRuby red appears in: primary buttons, FAB, the logo, active category pills, "urgent" status dots, and the mascot's shoes. Nowhere else. Restraint makes it pop.
