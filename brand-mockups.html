Tone of Voice

Warm, practical, never naggy

"You're on track this month with a healthy buffer."
"Budget surplus calculated: $1,240.00 remaining."
Like a friend who's good with money. Not a bank statement.
"Pest control is due — want to mark it done?"
"WARNING: Maintenance item overdue."
Gentle nudge, not an alarm. The app helps, it doesn't nag.
"Chicken stir-fry tonight. Missing 2 items."
"2/3 ingredients unavailable in shopping list."
Practical and specific. What to do, not what's wrong.
"Your rego expires Thursday — one less thing to forget."
"Vehicle registration expiry approaching. Action required."
Human language. The app is on your team.

Specifications

Border radiusCards: 14px · Pills: 99px · Modals: 20px · Inputs: 10px · FAB: 50%
Spacing4 · 8 · 12 · 16 · 20 · 24 · 32px
ShadowsCards: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.04) · Hover: 0 6px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.07) · FAB: 0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.2)
GradientsPrimary: #0891b2 → #7c3aed · Wallet: #059669 → #10b981 · Plan: #0284c7 → #0891b2 · Home: #7c3aed → #8b5cf6
Background#f0f9ff (ice blue) — warmer than white, cooler than cream
Status coloursRed #ef4444 (overdue) · Amber #f59e0b (due soon) · Green #10b981 (on track)
IconsLucide-style stroke, 2px weight. No emojis in core UI. Emojis in content labels only.
