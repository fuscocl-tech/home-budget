Settings & Feature Summary
Settings — Kids & Routines

👧 Kids & Routines

Completion reset time for routines and chores

Routine reset time

Completed tasks reset each day at this time. Default is midnight.

📋
Adult routines
Step-by-step daily routines with progress bars, streaks, drag-to-reorder, and 90-day history. My Routines tab is private; completions live on the routine record.
💡
Suggested steps
3 contextual suggestions shown per routine (expand for more). One tap adds the step. For child routines an edit button lets the adult set points before adding.
👥
Share & join
Routines can be shared with household members. Recipients can duplicate to their own list or join to stay in sync. Joined routines show a 🔗 badge and can't be edited.
👧
Children's routines
Managed from the Children's tab. Assign to one or multiple kids with inline chips. Per-child progress bars and streaks visible at a glance. Points per step + bonus on completion.
⏰
Time-aware (children)
Active routines are fully interactive. Non-active ones show 🔒 lock badge and disable all taps until the time window opens (triggerTime + 6 hrs).
👁
Adult read-only view
Parents can peek at any child's Today screen from Kids Zone. Dark "Read-only" bar at top, no sign-out button, all interactive buttons disabled.
🔔
Prize notifications
When a parent approves or declines a prize request, the child sees a green or red notification banner next login. One tap dismisses it.
🕛
Configurable reset
Choose when tasks reset — midnight, 4am, 5am, or 6am — in Settings → Kids & Routines. History preserved for streaks.
