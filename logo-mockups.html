Recommendation

If you want one to start with: 1, 4, or 9.

1 (charcoal) is the safest and most professional. 4 (single red 'o') keeps a hint of brand without competing with the rest of the UI. 9 (ring) is the most ownable — also gives you a tiny standalone logomark for the app icon, share images, and favicons.

To swap the logo in the app, lift the chosen variant into index.html and www/index.html at the .app-logo span — and tell me which one. Nothing has changed in the actual app.
