The R Shield
Robin's chest emblem — the iconic R inside a shield — reimagined as an app icon. Instantly recognisable, bold at every size, and carries the full weight of the Batman & Robin reference without spelling it out. Premium and confident.

The shield tapers to a point at the bottom — a nod to direction and purpose. You know where you're headed. Robin's got the plan.

The Caped Robin Bird
A robin bird wearing a cape — playful, warm, and instantly charming. The red breast of the bird doubles as Robin's signature colour. The cape sweeps behind, hinting at the sidekick connection without being heavy-handed.

This is the friendliest of the three — the one that feels like a companion, not a tool. It has mascot potential; you'd put this on a t-shirt.

Concept C
The Signal
Like the Bat-Signal but for Robin — a masked Robin figure lit from above, surrounded by signal arcs. The mask across the eyes is unmistakably the sidekick. The utility belt in gold anchors the bottom.

This is the most dramatic and premium of the three. It says: when life gets chaotic, you call Robin. The signal arcs also subtly reference notifications and alerts — your life planner pinging you into action.
"When life gets chaotic — call Robin."
"Fighting chaos, one plan at a time."
"Always on call."
"To the rescue, every day."
