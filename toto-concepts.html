The Full Body
A full-body Cairn Terrier side profile — tail up, ears pricked, facing forward. Scruffy charcoal coat, four sturdy little legs, and that classic Toto silhouette straight out of the 1939 film.

The emerald green collar with checkmark tag is the to-do wink — Emerald City meets your to-do list. Clean outline on white. Warm and solid on dark. Scales beautifully from hero to favicon.

The Paw + Check
A paw print where one toe is a checkmark — the to-do pun hiding in plain sight. Clean, minimal, works perfectly at any size. Clever without trying too hard.

The emerald green checkmark toe is the only colour contrast — it draws the eye straight to the meaning. Functional as an icon, fun as a brand mark. The kind of logo people notice and smile at.

The Running Toto
Toto mid-trot, tail up, carrying a to-do list in his wake. Full of personality and movement — this is a mascot as much as a logo. The curled tail signals joy and energy. The notepad says exactly what the app does.

The most illustrative and storytelling of the three — best for social media, onboarding screens, and brand moments. Scales down to a clean silhouette at small sizes.
