Recommended: Combine A + C

Use the Focus Card for the single most urgent item (with snooze/dismiss). Below it, use the Timeline for everything else grouped by time horizon. This gives you:

  • Focus Card — one big thing that demands attention, impossible to miss
  • Timeline below — "This week" and "Coming up" for everything else, scannable
  • Green "all clear" when the deck is empty — feels great
  • Snooze on the focus card — "remind me tomorrow" pushes it to tomorrow
  • The Today greeting sits above both — always there, always personal

Plus Banners (D) for context-aware alerts on other screens — like showing the grocery budget warning only when you're on the Budget page.
